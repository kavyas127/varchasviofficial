ABOUT US

Welcome to Varchasvi Arts, Crafts & Events, where we passionately celebrate the rich heritage of Indian handicrafts in the heart of Karnataka. Our curated collection showcases meticulously crafted treasures that embody the essence of our culture, each piece reflecting authenticity preserved by skilled artisans using age-old techniques. Offering a diverse range from vibrant textiles to intricately carved wooden sculptures, we cater to every taste while prioritizing sustainability through eco-friendly materials and practices. By choosing us, you directly contribute to the livelihoods of local artisans and their families, fostering community support. Born from a love for craftsmanship, Varchasvi Arts, Crafts & Events is a family-owned business dedicated to showcasing the hidden gems of our region and fostering connections between customers and artisans. Visit our cozy store to experience the scent of sandalwood mingling with vibrant silk sarees, and let us take you on a journey through Karnataka's artistic heritage. Thank you for supporting local artisans and being a part of our story!
